Red Dune Spicy Wings

Protein Selection: Earth-imported chicken or Mars-grown plant-based protein. Marination: Wings soak in a spicy Mars chili glaze for 12 hours, infusing heat and depth. Slow Roasting: Cooked in an infrared oven that evenly distributes heat. Fiery Glaze Finish: Brushed with an extra layer of Martian chili sauce before serving. Paired with Cosmic Blue Dipping Sauce: A cooling blend of hydroponic yogurt and Martian berry extract.
